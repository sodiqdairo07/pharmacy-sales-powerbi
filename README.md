European Pharmacy Chain — Sales & Profitability Analysis

 About This Project
I took on the OnyxData monthly data challenge where I was given a 
real-world pharmacy sales dataset covering a European chain operating 
across 8 countries. The goal was to dig into the data and build a 
report that helps commercial leaders, regional managers, and finance 
teams make smarter decisions.

What I Used
- Microsoft Power BI (report building and visualisation)
- DAX (custom measures and calculations)
- Power Query (data transformation and cleaning)
- Excel (source data)

What I Built
The report has 6 pages, each answering a specific business question:

1. Executive Summary:  A quick snapshot of overall business health
2. Sales Trends Over Time: How revenue is growing year on year
3. Geographic Performance: Which countries and regions are leading
4. Pharmacy Performance: What separates top pharmacies from the rest
5. Product & Category Analysis: Which products drive revenue vs profit
6. Promotional Analysis : Are promotions actually working?

 What I Found
- Germany is the strongest market bringing in €1.57M in revenue
- Wellness products have the best margins at 33.6% despite not being 
  the top revenue category — a hidden profit driver
- Urban pharmacies generate nearly 3x more revenue than Rural ones
- Only 10.5% of all sales were promotional — suggesting the business 
  relies heavily on organic demand
- 2025 is outperforming 2024 across every quarter showing steady growth
